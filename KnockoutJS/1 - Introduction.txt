
1) Headline features:

1a) Elegant dependency tracking 

- automatically updates the right parts of your UI whenever your data model changes.

1b) Declarative bindings 

- a simple and obvious way to connect parts of your UI to your data model. You can construct a complex dynamic UIs easily using arbitrarily nested binding contexts.

1c) Trivially extensible 

- implement custom behaviors as new declarative bindings for easy reuse in just a few lines of code.
